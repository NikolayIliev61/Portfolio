About me

Hi! I am 20 years old, Multimedia Design student at Ucn Aalborg. I love the challenges that the front-end profession provides, and this motivates me ,even more, to specialize in the field.

Project-01

Aalborg 89ers.

Aalborg 89ers is an American football club. Their website is poorly structured and this makes difficult for users to navigate around and find the information they need. I was part of a team of four and we worked on organizing the content, creating a user-centered design to provide better user experiences.

Project-02

Svalegarden Mest

Svalgarden Mest is a company that provides catering services. Their website is the main source of attracting new clients. However, the website content was poorly structured and was lack of information. I worked in a team of four where our goal was to provide Svalgarden Mest with an updated design of their website which can lead to a better user experience.

Project-03

Refood

Refood is an application(prototype) that allows people to track their food waste and keep them informed about the problem. I was in a team of three and we had to work with wicked problems. Refood is one solution based on creating values for a group of users instead of functionalities.
